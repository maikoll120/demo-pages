# Pool Example Terms & Conditions v2

## 1. Pool Coverage

This pool provides coverage for specific digital assets and smart contract interactions. Coverage is provided on a parametric basis with automatic payouts.

## 2. Eligibility

To be eligible for coverage under this pool, participants must:

- Hold the required minimum stake
- Maintain active participation
- Meet verification requirements

## 3. Limitations

Coverage under this pool is subject to:

- Maximum coverage limits
- Specific asset restrictions
- Protocol-specific conditions
