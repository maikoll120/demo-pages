## How does my investment earn returns?
Your investment earns returns based on the APY shown on your stake. Returns accrue over the investment period until maturity.

## What happens at maturity?
At maturity, you can withdraw your principal plus accrued rewards. The maturity date is shown on your stake detail.

## What are the early withdrawal penalties?
Early withdrawals before maturity may incur penalties. Withdrawals after maturity have no penalties.
