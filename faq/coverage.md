## Can I cancel my policy?
Policy cancellation is not possible through the dashboard. Please contact our support team for assistance with cancellation procedures.

## What happens when I reach my annual coverage limit?
Once you reach your annual coverage limit, you'll need to wait until the next policy period begins.
