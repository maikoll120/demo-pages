## How do I file a claim?
To file a claim, click the plus button at the top of the dashboard to start the process. Please note that you must have an active coverage policy before you can file a claim.

## What documents do I need to submit with my claim?
You'll need to provide detailed invoices that clearly list all medical procedures performed and their associated costs.

## How will I receive my claim payment?
Claim payments are processed and sent to your registered beneficiary address. You can view payment details and transaction history in the claim history section above.
